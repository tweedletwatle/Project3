Fullerton College
David spent a year and a half here

David attended Fullerton college to run track for legendary high jumper Doug Nordquist. He ran track his first year there but after a back injury decided to retire. For the first time since 8th grade, David was able to concentrate exclusively on his studies and was amazed at how successful he was without trying to juggle athletics at the same time. It was during this time David was employed at Disneyland working on the Submarine Voyage and Autopia.

University of Notre Dame
David attended Notre Dame for two and a half years

David transferred to Notre Dame from Fullerton College. While he was worried about the reputation for brutal winters, David experienced very mild winters during his time there. Some popular activites at Notre Dame are bookstore basketball which is the largest 5 on 5 outdoor basketball tournament in the world, watching Notre Dame's football and basketball teams, and attending Bengal Bouts which is an event where students box in order to raise money for the poor in Bangladesh. He studied Finance there.
